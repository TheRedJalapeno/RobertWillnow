About Robert Willnow

Robert Willnow is an American science fiction author known for his complex world-building and thought-provoking narratives. His stories explore the far reaches of human potential, alien civilizations, and futuristic technologies. Robert's writing delves into the depths of the human condition, often blending hard science fiction with philosophy.

Hemmer

Written By Victors, book 4

Professor Dr JG Hemmer is a reclusive specialist in the area of genomics. His story is not his to tell. Dive into a thrilling near-future science fiction adventure through a dystopian vision so close to our world you might see your house from there. From Rotterdam to Wolfsburg Germany, through the Earth-bound Tyson Station all the way to Venus, we have a chance to integrate into a variety of cultures along the way. What is the core conflict between Earth and the Spacers? Who struck first and why? What kind of weapons do you use in a pressurized space ship? Will Jamie ever find a job?

Hemmer is a fast-paced and funny scifi action adventure from acclaimed author Robert Willnow, clocking in as the fourth installment in the Written By Victors series.
